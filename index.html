About Caleb

A leader shaped by service, integrity, and commitment to the people

Caleb George Oguta represents a new generation of bold, accessible, and accountable leadership rooted in community needs and driven by real results.

Personality

Caleb is approachable, firm, focused, and development-oriented. He believes leadership must be visible, compassionate, and answerable to the people. He values listening, teamwork, and solutions that uplift every corner of the constituency.

Leadership Style

His leadership style is grounded in consultation, transparency, inclusivity, and decisive action. He seeks to unite generations, empower the youth, support women and families, and champion opportunities for everyone.

Vision & Mission

A clear promise for transformation

The campaign is built on inclusive growth, responsible use of public resources, and measurable development that reaches ordinary citizens.

Vision

To build a prosperous, secure, united, and empowered constituency where every person has a fair chance to thrive, every family can access opportunity, and every voice matters in leadership.

Mission

To provide servant leadership that delivers development, accountability, dignity, and practical solutions through partnership with the people, institutions, youth, women, farmers, workers, and business communities.

Manifesto

The people’s agenda for a stronger constituency

This manifesto focuses on the areas that directly affect everyday life: jobs, education, healthcare, agriculture, infrastructure, and good governance.

PILLAR 01

Education First

Expand bursary access, strengthen school infrastructure, support staffing advocacy, and promote equal learning opportunities for all children.

PILLAR 02

Youth Empowerment

Create pathways for jobs, internships, digital skills, innovation hubs, sports development, and youth-led enterprise support.

PILLAR 03

Healthcare Access

Strengthen local health facilities, improve maternal and child care, and advocate for better equipment, medicines, and staffing.

PILLAR 04

Roads, Water & Power

Push for improved roads, safe water access, rural electrification, and better infrastructure that unlocks local economic growth.

PILLAR 05

Agriculture & Trade

Support farmers with training, inputs, value chains, storage, market linkages, and stronger local business environments.

PILLAR 06

Transparent Leadership

Promote accountability, regular public engagement, responsible oversight, and participatory development planning.
