About Caleb

Caleb George Oguta is a visionary leader, committed to transforming South Nyanza through inclusive development, youth empowerment, and accountable leadership. With a strong background in leadership, governance, and community service, Caleb stands for integrity, progress, and unity.

Vision

To build a prosperous, inclusive, and empowered South Nyanza where every citizen has equal opportunity to succeed.

Mission

To deliver people-centered leadership that promotes development, transparency, job creation, and improved livelihoods for all residents.

Leadership Values

  • Integrity and accountability
  • Service to the people
  • Transparency in governance
  • Inclusivity and unity
  • Innovation and progress

Manifesto

Education

Improve school infrastructure, support bursaries, and enhance access to quality education.

Youth Empowerment

Create job opportunities, support innovation, and invest in skills development programs.

Healthcare

Strengthen health facilities, improve service delivery, and ensure affordable healthcare.

Infrastructure

Develop roads, improve water supply, and enhance rural electrification.

Agriculture

Support farmers with subsidies, training, and better market access.

Good Governance

Promote transparency, fight corruption, and ensure accountable leadership.

Background & Qualifications

Caleb George Oguta holds qualifications in leadership, governance, and development. He has extensive experience working with communities, leading initiatives that improve livelihoods, and advocating for youth and economic empowerment.
